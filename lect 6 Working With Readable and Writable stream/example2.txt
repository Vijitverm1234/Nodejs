Global warming is the increase in the average temperature of the Earth, and it's caused by a number of factors, including: 
Greenhouse effect
Certain gases in the atmosphere trap heat from the sun, similar to how a greenhouse traps heat. 
Fossil fuels
Burning coal, oil, and natural gas by industries and cars releases greenhouse gases that contribute to global warming. 
Deforestation
Destroying tropical forests contributes to global warming.
